Ingredients Instructions

Prep Time: 2 hours, 30 mins Cook: 5 mins Yield: 20-24 truffles

Ingredients

  • 300 g good-quality dark chocolate, 70% cocoa solids
  • 300 ml double cream
  • 50 g unsalted butter
  • Coconut powderi unsweetened cocoa powder, or shelled pistachio nuts, freeze-dried raspberries as you wish
  • vanilla extract
  • optional: salt

Instructions

  1. Chop the chocolate and tip into a large bowl
  2. Put the cream and butter into a sausepen and heat until the butter melts and the cream reaches simmering point.
  3. Remove from heat, then pour over the chocolate. Stir the chocolateand cream together until you have a smooth mixture.
  4. Add any flavourings to the truffele mix at this stage.
  5. Cool and chill for at least 4 hrs.
  6. To shape the truffes, dip a melon baller in hot water and scoop up balls of the mixture, the drop the truffles onto greaseproof paper.
  7. Roll the truffles between your palms.
  8. Try: shelled pistachio nuts in cocoa powder.
  9. Spoon the extra chocolate over -as you wish- the truffle until well-coated then chill.
  10. Keep in the fridge for three hours
